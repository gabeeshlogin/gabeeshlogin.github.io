Everything you need to know.

Reformer Pilates uses a specialised machine with a sliding carriage, springs, and straps to create resistance and support. It's far more versatile than mat work — you can do hundreds of exercises targeting every muscle group, with resistance you can adjust precisely. It's particularly effective for rehabilitation because the machine supports your body while still challenging it.
