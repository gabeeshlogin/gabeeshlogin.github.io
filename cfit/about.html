About Christina

Hi, I'm Christina.

Your Goals. Your Pace. Your Way.

I believe that movement has the power to change your life — not just your body. For me, the real magic of fitness lies in the mental clarity, confidence, and joy it brings, far beyond the numbers on a scale.

With 10 years of experience in the fitness industry, I've learned that there is no one-size-fits-all approach. What works for one person won't work for another, and that is exactly how it should be. Whether we are building functional power through Strength Training, finding balance on the Reformer Pilates carriage, or boosting energy with Circuit Training, our focus will always be on what makes you feel amazing.
